Deity Profiles

神明简介

Bodhisattva of Compassion

慈悲的菩萨

Guanyin (Avalokiteśvara)

观音

Attributes:

属性:

Compassion, mercy, kindness

慈悲、仁慈、善良

Mantra:

咒语:

Om Mani Padme Hum

嗡嘛呢叭咪吽

Significance:

重要性:

Guanyin is believed to hear the cries of all beings and to offer compassion and relief from suffering.

观音被认为能听到所有生灵的呼喊，并提供慈悲和减轻痛苦。

Bodhisattva of Wisdom

智慧的菩萨

Manjushri

文殊菩萨

Attributes:

属性:

Wisdom, understanding, insight

智慧、理解、洞察

Mantra:

咒语:

Om A Ra Pa Ca Na Dhih

嗡啊啦啪啊那底

Significance:

重要性:

Manjushri is revered as the embodiment of the wisdom of all the Buddhas. He is often invoked for wisdom and clarity in understanding the Dharma (Buddhist teachings).

文殊菩萨被尊崇为所有佛的智慧化身。他经常被祈求以获得理解佛法（佛教教义）的智慧和清晰度。

Buddha of Infinite Light

无量光佛

Amitabha

阿弥陀佛

Attributes:

属性:

Infinite light, infinite life, compassion

无限的光、无限的生命、慈悲

Mantra:

咒语:

Namo Amituofo (Chinese), Namo Amitābhāya (Sanskrit)

南无阿弥陀佛(中文),Namo Amitābhāya(梵文)

Significance:

重要性:

Central figure in Pure Land Buddhism. Believed to have created a Pure Land known as Sukhavati, where beings can be reborn through faith and devotion to Amitabha. The focus is often on Amitabha's compassion and the vow to save all sentient beings.

净土宗的中心人物。被认为创造了一个名为极乐世界的净土，生灵通过对阿弥陀佛的信仰和虔诚可以在那里得到重生。重点通常放在阿弥陀佛的慈悲和拯救所有众生的誓愿上。
